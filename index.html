News Summarizer

June 2020 - Current
Technology: Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, NewsAPI, MS Azure Cognitive Systems, MongoDB, IBM Watson

  From the beginning of 2020, I would've gotten surprised a few times a month by the news. For example, when the news about Yemen humanitarian crisis came out, I was very dissapointed that I had no idea about it happening until the situation became a world wide news. After that, I tried to follow the news more often, but the problem that came up was that learning about the news happening in every country was very time consuming, and translating the news to English was a big problem. That is when I came up with the idea of NewsSummarizer.

  In the project NewsSummarizer, I pull news from all around the world every 12 hours, and translate it using MSAzure to a user-input language. I came up with an algorithm in which the news gets summarized into 5 sentences or less, and commit the sentences and other informations about it in MongoDB noSQL database to use in the frontend. Furthermore, I am also analyzing the tone of the news using IBM Watson in order to give the user a general knowledge of the news.

  Currently, I am done with the BackEnd of my website, and I am working on the FrontEnd to display the results to the user.

  Using my project, I have already been more up to date on the news around the world, without having to spend hours on looking up different news sources and reading more about what's happening in the world.
